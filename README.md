---

##  Project Overview

**Pin Your Spot** is a mobile application designed to assist travelers, students, and professionals in navigating unfamiliar cities with ease. The app focuses on tourism and daily travel by providing navigation, destination alerts, travel history, and location-based recommendations. Its primary goal is to ensure users reach their destinations on time without missing stops, while also discovering nearby attractions and essential places.

---

##  Vision & Objectives

* Help users navigate efficiently in unfamiliar locations
* Provide destination-based alerts before reaching the stop
* Recommend popular and nearby attractions
* Maintain travel history and analytics for users
* Deliver a simple, intuitive, and user-friendly travel experience

---

##  Target Users

The application is designed for a wide range of users, regardless of age or gender:

* **Travelers** exploring new cities or countries
* **Students** relocating for education and daily commuting
* **Professionals** traveling for work and temporary stays

### User Personas

* **Mikey (28, Traveler):** Explores tourist destinations and relies on navigation and destination alerts.
* **Asta (22, Student):** Needs help finding universities, housing, grocery stores, and restaurants.
* **Gojo (25, Professional):** Looks for motels, transit routes, and eateries in a new country.

---

##  Product Niche

Pin Your Spot differentiates itself from existing applications through its **destination alarm feature**, which alerts users when they are within a predefined distance from their destination.

* Unlike **Google Maps**, which only alerts upon arrival
* Unlike **Alarm-Me**, which lacks navigation features

Pin Your Spot combines **navigation + destination alerts + recommendations** in a single application.

---

##  Features & Functionalities

* User registration and secure login
* Route navigation and ETA calculation
* Destination range setting with alert notifications
* Location-based tourist suggestions
* Travel history and analytics
* User profile management
* Password update and persistent login

---

##  Technology Stack

* **Backend & Database:** Firebase Firestore
* **Authentication:** Firebase Authentication
* **APIs:** Google Maps API (Navigation, Places, Directions)
